# On Building Tools That Last

*March 28, 2026*

Good tools are invisible. They work reliably, require minimal maintenance, and solve real problems. Yet building lasting tools is often overlooked in favor of flashy demonstrations.

## Why Tools Matter

In science, we often prioritize the discovery over the infrastructure. But without good tools, discoveries can't be reproduced or built upon by others.

## What Makes a Good Tool

- **Simplicity**: Easy to use and understand
- **Documentation**: Clear instructions for users
- **Maintainability**: Code that's easy to update
- **Testing**: Confidence that it actually works

## The Payoff

When you invest in sustainable tools, you enable others to do better science. That's a legacy worth building.
