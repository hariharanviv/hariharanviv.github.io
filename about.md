# Vivek Hariharan

**Ph.D. student, Johns Hopkins University**

Hi — I'm Vivek Hariharan, a Ph.D. student studying HIV-1 persistence in the [Siliciano lab](https://silicianolab.jhu.edu/). My research focuses on the immune response to HIV-1 and creating high-throughput assays to measure individual immune responses. A related side project on non-suppressible viremia was published recently: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41564-025-02135-z

I'm finishing my Ph.D. in the coming months and plan to start a postdoctoral position in [David Liu's lab](https://www.liugroup.us/).

## About this site

This site is a place for short, data-driven essays and notes from my research journey. If you find something interesting, please reach out.

### A short note on AI

AI tools are increasingly used in scientific writing; I generally avoid using AI to generate narrative content here (aside from minor proofreading), but I do use computational tools for data analysis and visualization.
