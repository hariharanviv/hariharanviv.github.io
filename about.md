# Vivek Hariharan

**Ph.D. student, Johns Hopkins University**

Hi — I'm Vivek Hariharan, a Ph.D. student studying HIV-1 persistence in the [Siliciano lab](https://silicianolab.jhu.edu/). My research focuses on the immune response to HIV-1 and creating high-throughput assays to measure immune responses. I also worked a bit on non-suppressible viremia, a clinical phenomenon where people with HIV-1 cannot suppress their viral load despite full adherence to antiretrovirals. We recently [published](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41564-025-02135-z) on new mechanisms of non-suppressible viremia, highlighting two cases from the Hopkins clinic. 

I'm finishing my Ph.D. in the coming months and plan to start a postdoctoral position in [David Liu's lab](https://www.liugroup.us/).

This site is a place for short, data-driven essays and notes from my research journey. If you find something interesting, please reach out.
